HOW IT WORKS

Book your own flights and use the Golf Voyager platform to choose the best priced package of golf, hotel and transfers or any combination, there of.

All our packages are automatically generated by our system. You can customise any aspect of the packages to your liking in 3 easy steps:

  1. Enter your details into the search box and click on ‘Select’ to choose a package that you like, or just simply click on ‘Select’ to choose any of the highlighted packages.
  2. Make whatever changes you want in the basket by using the add, remove and edit buttons.
  3. Proceed to check out.

Even if the hotel and golf courses that you are looking for are not in any of the packages shown, click select and on any of the packages. You can then add, remove and edit any element to build your perfect package.

If you need any help we are available on LiveChat, Phone +35361293533 or Email - info@golfvoyager.com.
